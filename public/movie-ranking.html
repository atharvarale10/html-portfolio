The Best Movies I watched in 2024

A Review by Atharva

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind (2004)

A brilliant Rom-Com with a perfect blend of sci-fi which would resonate with the taste of both rom-com and sci-fi lovers.

Se7en

A brilliant Thriller with an intriguing plot that keeps you on the edge.

Laapata Ladies

An amazing Bollywood movie which feels very natural and raw. Amazing display of acting as well as a well executed storyline.
